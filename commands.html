Command List

Music Module

@Botname play [Keyword/Link] -- Plays the Song
@Botname stop -- Stops Playing Music but stays in the channel
@Botname skip -- Skips to the next Song
@Botname nowplaying/np -- Shows the current played Song
@Botname vol/volume -- Changed the Volume of the Music
@Botname showqueue -- Lists the current queue
